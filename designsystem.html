Spacing Semantics — Gestalt Proximity

micro 2px & xs 4px — Intra-element
Dots, icon stacks, badge icon-text. Signals "these are one unit." Used within a single atomic component (e.g. icon + symbol in .s-syms, tab container rows).
2xs 6px — Icon-text inline
Gap between an icon/dot and its label inside pill buttons and chips. 4px is too tight (letters collide with icon visually), 8px is too loose. 6px fills the half-step.
sm 8px & md 12px — Within-component
Related items inside a card or row (icon to text, label to value). Most common range — these tokens handle the majority of spacing. Everything that "belongs together but is still two things."
lg 16px & xl 20px — Section breathing room
Padding inside panels, gaps between rows in a list, space around section headers. Creates the margin of safety between groups so the eye can parse them as separate.
2xl 24px & 3xl 32px — Between sections
Separates distinct content blocks (e.g. detail panel sections, compare score vs notes grid). Use sparingly — only in parent gap on containers like .detail-inner, .cmp-results.
4xl 48px — Page-level framing
Top/bottom page padding, major section breaks. Should stay rare — overuse collapses the distinction between section-level and page-level space.
